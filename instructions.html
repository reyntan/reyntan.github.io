Instructions

Dear participant , thank you for participating! Please read the instructions before the experiment.

Process

The steps for the experiment are as follows:

  1. Before you begin, you will undergo a pre-trial after you press 'Next' to familiarise yourself with both copy-paste techniques.
  2. Next, the actual experiment involves 27 trials of copy-paste tasks per technique. There will be a one minute break in between techniques.
  3. Thus, there will be 54 trials for one experimental set. You will repeat the set 3 times in total.

What you'll do

The techniques

Traditional

Press CTRL - C + CTRL - V to copy and paste selected text in the windows.

AutoComPaste

Begin by typing the text in the Text Editor window. A list of suggestions will drop-down.

Pasting text
Use up and down arrow keys to select desired text and press Enter.

Pasting sentences
Use right arrow key to paste the next sentence after the current sentence.
